### 1.3 Core insight (SDC мқҳ intent)
**Meta мҷҖ post-meta лҠ” м—ӯн• мқҙ лӢӨлҘҙлҜҖлЎң signal мқҳ л°©н–ҘлҸ„ лӢ¬лқјм•ј н•ңлӢӨ**:

- **Post-meta нҶ нҒ°** (answer commit): decoy-conditioned teacher л¶„нҸ¬м—җм„ң **repel** вҖ” мҳӨлӢө manifold м—җм„ң л°Җм–ҙлӮҙлҗҳ gold лҘј м§Ғм ‘ лӘЁл°©н•ҳм§Җ м•ҠмқҢ

### 1.4 Why "answer imitation" мқҙ м•„лӢҢк°Җ (structurally)
л„Ө м„ұм§Ҳмқҙ лҸҷмӢң м„ұлҰҪ:

1. Student rollout мқҙ loss support лҘј м •мқҳ (teacher лҠ” мғҲ completion м•Ҳ л§Ңл“Ұ)
2. Advantage sign мқҖ нҷҳкІҪ reward к°Җ кІ°м • (teacher лҠ” magnitude л§Ң мЎ°м Ҳ)
3. Meta attract: gold-informed л¶„нҸ¬мҷҖмқҳ log-ratio лҘј advantage magnitude м—җ кіұн•Ё вҖ” token мқ„ м§Ғм ‘ copy н•ҳм§Җ м•ҠмқҢ
4. **Post-meta repel: wrong-informed л¶„нҸ¬м—җм„ң л©Җм–ҙм§Җкё°л§Ң н•Ё вҖ” gold нҶ нҒ°мқ„ imitate н•ҳм§Җ м•ҠмқҢ**

## 2. Hypotheses

### 2.1 H-SDC-1: SDC reduces all four collapse symptoms vs N3
**Claim**: Same compute budget (300 step, SEED=42), SDC outperforms N3 on all four В§3 collapse metrics simultaneously.

| Metric | SDC target | N3 expected | Pass criterion |
|---|---|---|---|
| Wrap rate | $\geq 95\%$ | $\leq 92\%$ | $\geq 95$ AND $+ \geq 3$ pp over N3 |
| AIME truncation | $\leq 20\%$ | $\sim 30\%$ | $\leq 20$ AND $-\geq 10$ pp |
| Confidence entropy | $\geq 0.5$ bits | $\leq 0.3$ bits | $\geq 0.5$ AND $+\geq 0.2$ bits |
| $\Delta H_{\text{meta}\pm 5}$ | $\geq +0.150$ nats | $\leq 0$ | $\geq +0.150$ AND $+\geq 0.15$ nats |

**Statistical test**: McNemar paired test on binary wrap/truncation, Wilcoxon signed-rank on continuous entropy/О”H, family-wise Holm-Bonferroni at $\alpha = 0.05$ over 4 tests.

**Falsifier**: Any of the four conditions unmet вҶ’ H-SDC-1 fails. Downgrade to "partial improvement" if 2-3 of 4 pass.

### 2.2 H-SDC-2: Post-meta repel magnitude controls diversity
**Claim**: Varying $\lambda_{\text{post}} \in \{0.0, 0.1, 0.3, 0.5\}$ produces monotonically increasing confidence entropy (within $\pm 0.05$) up to the first saturation point.

**Pass criterion**: Pearson correlation $r(\lambda_{\text{post}}, H_{\text{conf}}) \geq 0.7$ in the monotonic regime (pre-saturation).

**Falsifier**: Non-monotonic relation OR saturation at $\lambda_{\text{post}} = 0.1$ (too weak to separate).

### 2.3 H-SDC-3: Repel does not imitate gold
**Claim**: Student's post-meta token distribution after SDC training is **not** closer to gold-answer distribution than it was after meta-only training (M1).

**Method**: Compute KL$(\pi_{\text{student}}^{\text{post}} \| \pi_{\text{gold-conditioned}}^{\text{post}})$ for SDC vs M1 on held-out set. SDC should be $\geq$ M1 (or not significantly smaller).

**Pass criterion**: $|\text{KL}_{\text{SDC}} - \text{KL}_{\text{M1}}| \leq 0.1$ nats (not systematically closer).

**Falsifier**: $\text{KL}_{\text{SDC}} < \text{KL}_{\text{M1}} - 0.2$ nats вҶ’ SDC is secretly imitating gold via repel вҶ’ structural claim (1.4-4) falsified.

### 2.4 H-SDC-4: Region split is necessary (not just combined signal)
**Claim**: A control variant "SDC-uniform" (apply the same attract+repel signal uniformly to meta AND post-meta) underperforms SDC-split on H-SDC-1 metrics.

**Pass criterion**: SDC-split вүҘ SDC-uniform on wrap AND truncation by $\geq 2$ pp.

**Falsifier**: SDC-split and SDC-uniform within 1 pp on both metrics вҶ’ split is not doing work, attract+repel combination alone suffices.

### 2.5 H-SDC-5: SDC aligns with В§4 two-region sign condition
**Claim**: Post-meta tokens for which the anti-alignment condition $\gamma_{\text{post}} \cdot \Delta H_{\text{post}} < 0$ holds show larger $w_t^{\text{post}}$ (repel magnitude) than those where it holds weakly.

**Pass criterion**: Spearman rank correlation between $|\gamma_{\text{post}} \cdot \Delta H_{\text{post}}|$ and $|w_t^{\text{post}} - 1|$ $\geq 0.4$ over 10k held-out post-meta tokens.

**Falsifier**: Correlation $\leq 0.2$ or opposite sign вҶ’ SDC's repel does not track Prop 1 anti-alignment, so В§4 extension is not empirically supported.

## 3. Verification Protocol

### 3.1 Method specification
Split the advantage factor into three regions via disjoint masks:

$$
\hat A_t = A_i \cdot \left[(1 - \lambda) + \lambda \cdot \left( m_t^{\text{meta}} \cdot w_t^{\text{attr}} + m_t^{\text{post}} \cdot w_t^{\text{rep}} + m_t^{\text{body}} \cdot 1 \right)\right]
$$

where $m_t^{\text{meta}} + m_t^{\text{post}} + m_t^{\text{body}} = 1$ (disjoint coverage of completion tokens), and

$$
w_t^{\text{attr}} = \exp\left(\text{sign}(A_i) \cdot \text{clip}(\log P_{T^+}(y_t) - \log P_S(y_t), -10, 10)\right)
$$
$$
w_t^{\text{rep}} = \exp\left(-\text{sign}(A_i) \cdot \text{clip}(\log P_{T^-}(y_t) - \log P_S(y_t), -10, 10)\right)
$$

$T^+$ = teacher with gold answer prepended, $T^-$ = teacher with deterministic decoy prepended (existing N3 infrastructure, unchanged).

### 3.2 Mask definitions
- $m_t^{\text{meta}} = 1$ iff token index $t$ lies between `<|meta|>` and `<|/meta|>` (inclusive of boundary tokens)
- $m_t^{\text{post}} = 1$ iff token $t$ is after `<|/meta|>` and on/before the token containing `}` that closes the first `\boxed{...}` pattern
- $m_t^{\text{body}} = 1$ elsewhere (pre-meta reasoning + post-boxed trailing)

Invariant: exactly one of three masks is 1 per completion token. Verified in unit test `test_sdc_mask_disjoint`.

### 3.3 Training configuration
- Base: `contrastive_meta_rlsd.yaml` (v8 meta SFT student, num_rollouts=4, per_device=1)
- New: `lambda_meta` and `lambda_post` (independent schedules)
  - `lambda_meta`: 0.5 вҶ’ 0 linear over 75 steps (same as current N3)
  - `lambda_post`: 0.1 вҶ’ 0.3 linear over 150 steps (ramp up; start weak to avoid format destruction)
- All other N3 hyperparameters unchanged (teacher sync 10 steps, clip_eps_low=0.2, clip_eps_high=0.28, decoy_seed=42)

### 3.4 Experimental plan
| Variant | Mask | Attract | Repel | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| M1 | meta | gold-teacher | вҖ” | RLSD baseline |
| N3 | meta | gold vs decoy | вҖ” | Meta contrastive (current) |
| **SDC-split** | meta + post | meta only | post-meta only | **H-SDC-1 primary** |
| SDC-uniform | meta + post | both | both | H-SDC-4 control |
| SDC-О»-sweep | meta + post | meta only | post-meta only | H-SDC-2 ($\lambda_{\text{post}} \in \{0.0, 0.1, 0.3, 0.5\}$) |

Primary run: 300 steps per variant, 2 seeds (42, 43). Ablation: 150 steps. Total ~8 runs Г— 4 GPU Г— 4h вүҲ 128 GPU-hr incremental budget on top of existing EAD plan.

### 3.5 Evaluation points
- Step 0 (SFT baseline), 50, 100, 150, 200, 250, 300
- 1,030 problems (GSM8K 500 + MATH 500 + AIME 30), max_tokens=4096
- Metrics: wrap rate, AIME truncation, stated confidence entropy, $\Delta H_{\text{meta}\pm 5}$, Mahalanobis $d_M$, MI$(M_c; Y|D)$, post-marker Cohen $d$, overall accuracy
- H-SDC-3 (gold-KL) computed once at step 300 on held-out 200 problems
- H-SDC-5 (Spearman) computed on 10k post-meta token pairs from step 300 rollouts

### 3.6 Falsification-driven stopping rule
If smoke test (30 step, 50 prompt) shows any of:
- Training loss diverges (>10Г— baseline within 20 steps)
- Wrap rate drops below SFT baseline by $\geq 10$ pp at step 30
- $\nabla_\theta$ NaN or $> 10^3$ in $\geq 5\%$ of steps

вҶ’ Halt, downgrade H-SDC hypothesis priority, revert to N3. No relaunch without plan revision.

## 4. Contrast with Prior Work

### 4.1 Contrastive LLM methods
| Method | Granularity | Region split | Direction | Distinction from SDC |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Contrastive Decoding (Li 2023) | inference | none | subtract amateur | Decoding only, no training signal |
| DoLa (Chuang 2024) | inference | none | layer-contrast | Decoding only |
| cDPO (2024) | training token | none | positive/negative models | Two separate models, no region split |
| DistiLLM-2 (ICML 2025) | training token | none | teacherвҶ‘ / studentвҶ“ | Response-uniform, no region split |
| RLCD (ICLR 2024) | training response | none | positive/negative prompts | Response-level, no token region |
| RLSD (2604.03128) | training token | none | privileged teacher | Global privileged, no region split |
| HDPO (2603.23871) | training token | none | privileged teacher | Single privileged, no pair, no region |
| SDPO (2601.20802) | training token | none | feedback-conditioned self | Feedback, no counterfactual pair, no region |
| N3 (ours, meta) | training token | meta only | gold vs decoy Bayes | No post-meta signal |
| **SDC (ours)** | **training token** | **meta vs post-meta** | **attract meta / repel post-meta** | **First region-specialized directional contrastive with asymmetric attract+repel** |

### 4.2 Novelty claim
The combination **{meta-attract + post-meta-repel + disjoint region masks}** is unpublished. The closest antecedents are:
- N3 (ours): same contrastive pair, meta-only
- cDPO: token-level contrast, response-uniform, two-model instead of two-context
- DistiLLM-2: token-level attract-repel (teacher up, student down), response-uniform

SDC's novelty = **region-specialized asymmetric directionality**, empirically motivated by В§3 two-region role diagnosis.

## 5. Implementation Path

### 5.1 Code changes (estimated ~200 LOC)
1. `src/training/meta_rlsd_data_pipeline.py`: compute `postmeta_mask` alongside existing `meta_mask`; disjoint by construction
2. `src/training/contrastive_meta_rlsd_trainer.py::_compute_per_token_advantage`: accept `postmeta_mask`, compute `w_t^rep`, combine into `hat_A`
3. `src/training/contrastive_meta_rlsd_trainer.py`: add `lambda_post` schedule separate from `lambda_meta`
4. `configs/contrastive_meta_rlsd.yaml`: add `variant: sdc`, `variant: sdc-uniform`, `lambda_post_init/final/warmup`
5. `src/training/rewards.py`: no change (meta_floor logic independent)
6. `tests/test_contrastive_meta_rlsd.py`: add 6 unit tests
   - `test_sdc_mask_disjoint`: all three masks sum to 1
   - `test_sdc_repel_direction`: repel magnitude flips sign with advantage sign
   - `test_sdc_lambda_post_schedule`: linear ramp 0.1вҶ’0.3 over 150 steps
   - `test_sdc_no_gold_imitation`: post-meta gradient does not match gold token gradient (controlled input)
   - `test_sdc_uniform_fallback`: when `variant=sdc-uniform`, both regions get attract+repel
   - `test_sdc_body_identity`: body tokens have $w_t = 1$ exactly
7. `scripts/driver_v5_m1_n3.sh`: add `VARIANT=sdc` and `VARIANT=sdc-uniform` dispatch
8. `scripts/stage_n3_e8.sh`: no change (data pipeline unchanged)

### 5.2 Smoke-critic-improve loop plan
- **Smoke**: 30-step run on 50 prompts; verify loss, wrap, NaN
- **Critic**: code-reviewer agent on diff, flag HIGH/MEDIUM
- **Improve**: apply critic fixes
- **Repeat** until 3 consecutive iterations with zero HIGH issues AND smoke passes

1. **If H-SDC-1 passes** (4 of 4 collapse metrics improve): SDC becomes primary method, В§5 reframed around region-specialized contrastive, submit as headline contribution
2. **If H-SDC-1 partial** (2-3 of 4): SDC enters В§5 as variant G, ablation plot highlights which collapse modes respond; Path A (diagnosis-centric) narrative
